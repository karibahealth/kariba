Kariba is a virtual family practice clinic taking care of all ages from babies to the most elderly. We are accepting patients for ongoing care as well as providing virtual “walk in” care.

We are happy to provide care to people visiting Vancouver or without MSP coverage.

Diagnosis and Treatment

We can assist with diagnosis and treatment of the majority of conditions that you would go to a traditional clinic with. We also offer ongoing care for more chronic conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, obesity, COPD etc and virtual routine baby check ups.

We cannot provide prescriptions for restricted substances as a virtual clinic. If your reason for booking is exclusively for this reason your appointment request will be declined. Our only limitations are — not being able to do yearly physicals and pap tests and skin biopsies. We do not offer emergency or urgent care services, if you require these please contact your local urgent care centre or emergency room.

FAQ

Yes, there are times when you won’t be able to access us directly or immediately, but we hope that this list of healthcare resources can provide a solution for you in these situations. Kariba Health is committed to being your family doctor and providing long term continued care.

What if I need something done in person that you don’t currently offer?
We will gladly refer you to a number of colleagues who will assist you with your needs and report back to Kariba Health with how to continue your ongoing care.
What happens at the weekend or if you are unavailable?
First, ask yourself whether your concern could wait until we are available? If so, please do book and we will happily address your concerns. However, we understand that sometimes things simply can’t wait. We don't offer 24/7 service but we are all lucky to live in Vancouver where there are some fantastic options.
Life Threatening
Do you think you are having a heart attack or stroke? Dial 911 immediately or have someone take you to your closest emergency room.
Urgent
Have you broken a leg, need stitches, having an asthma attack? Go to your local urgent care centre.
Mental Health Issue
Vancouver general hospital offers a fantastic service called Access and Assessment centre.
Walk in Clinic
For other non-urgent but important issues which you need, particularly over the weekend or holidays, consider visiting a walk-in clinic. For locations and wait times, visit Medimap Vancouver.
Lab Results
You can register to view any available lab results by logging on to MyHealth.

Accountability

As a team we work to be transparent, efficient and accountable. We ask the same from you as patients as you are an integral part of your health care team. Booking, results, follow up and referrals are all done through our online portal. You book your own appointment and choose the reason for the visit at that time. To keep us on time and efficient please book these correctly and be aware of our late cancellation and no-show policy. We aim to offer you the best in modern health care.

Cancellation Policy

We require 24 hour cancellation of any appointment. Cancellation and no-show fees are $50 and you will need to pay this before you are able to book again. More than 3 cancellations or no-shows will result in you being removed as a patient.
